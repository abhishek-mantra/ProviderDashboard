Use a local state array or individual booleans to track which FAQ is open. The five questions and answers are:
Question: "What happens when my credits run out?" — Answer: When a specific feature's credits are fully used, Extra Credits automatically step in to cover the overflow. If Extra Credits are also exhausted, your paid balance is charged to ensure uninterrupted service. You will never lose access to a feature mid-usage.
Question: "Do unused credits roll over to the next month?" — Answer: Free credits reset on your monthly billing cycle date and do not carry over. However, purchased credits never expire and remain in your account until fully used.
Question: "How are credits counted for each feature?" — Answer: Each feature deducts credits based on usage. For example, one session note with AI consumes 2 credits, one without AI consumes 1 credit, and one prescription with AI consumes 2 credits. You can view the full breakdown in the Overview tab.
Question: "Can I allocate specific credits to individual team members?" — Answer: Yes. Admins can set per-tool credit limits as a percentage of total credits to control usage and prevent any single tool from consuming the shared pool.
Question: "How do I purchase additional credits?" — Answer: You can purchase additional credits at any time by opening the Extra Credits accordion in the Overview tab, moving the slider to your desired amount, and clicking the Buy Credits button that appears.
